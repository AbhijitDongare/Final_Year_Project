ISL (INDIAN SIGN LANGUAGE) LEARNING is a
website to learn sign language alphabetically or 
body movement. It consists of hand movements, hand 
shapes as well as facial expressions, and lip patterns
to demonstrate what people want to say.
It helps deaf people to communicate with other people
easily.
I want to create this website for needy people who want 
to learn sign language
